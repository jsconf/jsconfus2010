Track B Registration

Track B is our "first-come, first-served" unconference component to JSConf. We provide a forum for you, the JSConf attendees to present your latest and greatest ideas. We understand that your innovation is what drives the community, so this is your chance to show your stuff. Below are the available time slots for JSConf on both Saturday and Sunday, if you have a talk you want to present, go ahead and pick your timeslot. We will be recording the Track B presentations this year, so before you sign up to present we require that you confirm to release the rights to the presentation so we can post it on the JSConf website.
