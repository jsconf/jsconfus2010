JSConf 2010

Dear JSConf Interested,

It is with a heavy heart that have to post this note, but we felt like we had to do it as soon as possible. Due to circumstances beyond our control, we have to cancel JSConf US for 2010. Unfortunately, we do not have the funds to return ticket prices to attendees.

What happened you might ask? We received an email from a Somalian programmer who wanted to go to JSConf, but had to deal with financing it in a special way. Impressively, his English was strangely acute and only had few issues - he even sent us a picture of his family. The picture is what got us by the heart strings. I mean look at this picture, the guy in the camouflage is Harold that is his brother. As mentioned in the photo, they always pose with fake weapons, just to keep appearances up - strange, but its cultural we guess.

Our first thought was that this seemed fishy, but we really wanted a JSConf Africa and this might be the way to that. So we worked with his "banker" in London who helped us with getting the funds for the ticket purchase into the JSConf bank account. The transaction went through and we issued his ticket.

Three days later, there was a call from our bank saying that the charge had been reversed and there were zero funds remaining in the account. We have be fraught for days, but the bank assured us that the issue would be resolved. It has been about 2 weeks now that we have been dealing with this issue and it does not appear to be coming to a positive end anytime soon, so unfortunately we will have to cancel JSConf for this year until we can get this resolved.

We are dreadfully sorry and hope that you can possibly understand,

The JSConf Team
